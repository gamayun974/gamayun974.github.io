Privacy Policy for Helicopter Hogs Hunting

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application Helicopter Hogs Hunting, developed by Gamayun Productions.

Helicopter Hogs Hunting is a mobile action game featuring helicopter-based hog hunting gameplay. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information Collection

1. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain types of data automatically, such as:

  • Device type and model
  • IP address and mobile operating system
  • Game usage data (e.g. session duration, levels completed)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID)
  • Crash and diagnostic logs

2. Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your data to personalize ads and improve performance. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more information.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or contact details.

Location Data

This app does not collect precise location information.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, except as necessary to:

  • Serve and optimize ads (e.g. AdMob)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety

All shared data is anonymized and aggregated where possible.

Data Retention

We retain analytics and usage data for up to 24 months. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

Opt-out Options

You can opt-out of personalized ads from Google by visiting your device settings and selecting “Opt out of Ads Personalization”.

You may also uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

Changes

This policy may be updated. We will post any changes on this page. Continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated policy.
